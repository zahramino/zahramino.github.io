Education and ChatGPT

Integrating NLP-based services like ChatGPT in education can raise concerns about students becoming dependent on it to solve assignments. However, I think with appropriate guidance, educators can leverage the capabilities of ChatGPT to enhance the students' learning experience. One strategy could be to encourage students to critically evaluate the output generated by ChatGPT and identify potential limitations, for example, in coding assignments. Due to the vastness of the internet, the data used to train models like ChatGPT may contain biases. Also, ChatGPT is not a real-time model; the current version has been trained on data before 2021. #chatgpt #education #nlp
